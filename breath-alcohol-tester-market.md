<p><p>The Breath Alcohol Tester market is segmented by technology. Fuel Cell Type testers utilize chemical reactions to measure alcohol concentration, offering high accuracy and reliability. Semiconductor Type testers use sensors to detect alcohol vapors, providing quick results but may have lower precision. Infrared Type testers analyze the absorption of infrared light by alcohol molecules, ensuring accuracy over a wide range of concentrations. Gas Chromatography Analysis Type testers separate and quantify substances, offering highly accurate results, typically used in professional settings and laboratories.</p></p>

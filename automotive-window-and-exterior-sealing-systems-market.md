<p><p>The automotive window and exterior sealing systems market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **EPDM Sealing Systems**: Made from ethylene propylene diene monomer, these systems offer excellent resistance to weathering, heat, and ozone, making them ideal for durability and longevity in vehicles.</p><p>2. **TPE/TPO Sealing Systems**: Thermoplastic elastomers (TPE) and thermoplastic olefins (TPO) provide flexibility, ease of processing, and recyclability, making them popular choices for modern automotive applications.</p><p>3. **PVC Sealing Systems**: Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) sealing systems are cost-effective and provide good sealing capabilities, though they may have limitations in heat resistance compared to EPDM and TPE/TPO options.</p></p>

<p><p>Connected street lights can be categorized into two main market types: wired and wireless. Wired systems utilize Power Line Communication (PLC) technology, where data is transmitted over existing electrical power lines, ensuring stable connectivity and lower latency. In contrast, wireless systems employ various communication technologies such as Wi-Fi, Zigbee, or cellular networks, offering flexibility and easier installation. Each approach has its benefits and challenges, influencing factors like installation costs, scalability, and overall network reliability in smart city implementations.</p></p>

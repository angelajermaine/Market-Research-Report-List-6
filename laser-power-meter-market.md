<p><p>The laser power meter market comprises various detector types, primarily including thermopile detectors, photodiode detectors, and pyroelectric energy sensors. Thermopile detectors measure power by converting thermal energy into an electrical voltage, making them suitable for high-power laser measurements. Photodiode detectors utilize semiconductor technology to provide fast response times and are ideal for low-power applications. Pyroelectric energy sensors detect sudden changes in energy, making them effective for pulsed lasers. Each type serves specific needs in industrial, scientific, and research applications.</p></p>

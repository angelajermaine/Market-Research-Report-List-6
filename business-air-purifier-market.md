<p><p>The business air purifier market encompasses several types designed to enhance indoor air quality. Activated carbon purifiers absorb odors and harmful gases, making them effective for commercial spaces. UV technology purifiers utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses, ensuring healthier environments. Ion purifiers release negatively charged ions that attach to pollutants, causing them to settle. Ozone generators produce ozone to neutralize odors and contaminants, though their safety can be a concern. Other market types include HEPA filters and electrostatic precipitators, offering diverse solutions for businesses.</p></p>

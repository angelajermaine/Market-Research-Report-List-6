<p><ul><li>Monopole</li><li>Twin Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cutting loop electrodes market primarily features two types: monopole and twin pole electrodes. Monopole electrodes consist of a single conductive loop, making them suitable for simpler cutting procedures. In contrast, twin pole electrodes feature two loops that enhance precision and efficiency in tissue cutting and ablation, allowing for improved thermal distribution. These distinctions cater to varying medical needs, with each type offering specific advantages in surgical procedures, influencing their utilization in the healthcare sector.</p></p>

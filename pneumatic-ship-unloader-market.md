<p><p>The Pneumatic Ship Unloader market features two primary types: mobile and floor track systems. Mobile pneumatic unloaders are versatile and can be relocated easily, making them suitable for different ship sizes and unloader requirements. Floor track systems, on the other hand, have a fixed installation that runs along a dedicated track, allowing for efficient unloading at a specific location. Both types leverage pneumatic technology to efficiently transfer bulk materials from ships to shore, enhancing productivity in port operations.</p></p>

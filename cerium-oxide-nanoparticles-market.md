<p><p>Cerium oxide nanoparticles are available in two primary market types: dispersion and powder. Dispersion refers to nanoparticles suspended in a liquid medium, facilitating easy application in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, coatings, and catalysis. Powder form, on the other hand, consists of dry nanoparticles, commonly used in abrasives, ceramics, and catalysts. Each type caters to specific requirements, with dispersion offering versatility in formulations and powder providing convenience for solid-state applications and bulk processing.</p></p>

<p><p>The patient plate market can be divided into two primary types: disposable and reusable. Disposable patient plates are designed for single-use, emphasizing convenience and hygiene, particularly in clinical settings. They help prevent cross-contamination and reduce cleaning time. In contrast, reusable patient plates are durable, made for multiple uses, and typically require thorough cleaning and sterilization between patients. While reusable options may have a lower long-term cost, disposable plates are often preferred for their ease of use and infection control benefits.</p></p>

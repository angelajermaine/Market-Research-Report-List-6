Continuous case packers operate in high-speed environments, handling a steady flow of products without interruption, ideal for mass production settings. In contrast, intermittent case packers function in a stop-and-go manner, accommodating varying production rates and product types, making them suitable for smaller batch operations or diverse product lines. Both types enhance efficiency in packaging while meeting different production needs and operational scales.</p></p>

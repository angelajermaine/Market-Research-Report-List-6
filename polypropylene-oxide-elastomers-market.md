<p><p>Polypropylene oxide elastomers can be categorized into two primary types: mechanical blend type and chemical graft type. Mechanical blend types consist of a combination of polypropylene oxide with other elastomers, resulting in enhanced flexibility and resilience. Chemical graft types involve the chemical modification of polypropylene oxide to introduce functional groups, improving compatibility with other materials and tailoring properties for specific applications. Both types are used in diverse industries, such as automotive, construction, and consumer goods, due to their versatile performance characteristics.</p></p>

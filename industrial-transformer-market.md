<p><p>The industrial transformer market is primarily categorized into oil-cooled and air-cooled transformers. Oil-cooled transformers utilize liquid insulation and cooling through oil, providing efficient heat dissipation and allowing for higher capacity and lower maintenance. They are often used in substations and heavy industries. Air-cooled transformers, on the other hand, rely on air for cooling, making them suitable for smaller applications and environments where space is limited. Both types serve essential roles in power distribution and energy management across various sectors.</p></p>

Low pressure valves are designed to relieve pressure at lower thresholds, commonly used in applications like water systems. High pressure valves are engineered for extreme conditions, safeguarding critical systems in industries like oil and gas. Each type ensures operational safety by preventing overpressure, thereby protecting equipment and reducing hazards.</p></p>

PVC is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and decent chemical resistance, suitable for less demanding environments. TPE combines the benefits of rubber and plastic, providing excellent elasticity and temperature resistance. Each material type serves distinct applications across industries, influencing performance and longevity.</p></p>

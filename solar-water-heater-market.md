<p><ul><li>Pressurized Solar Water Heater</li><li>Non Pressure Solar Water Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar water heater market comprises two main types: pressurized and non-pressurized systems. Pressurized solar water heaters utilize a closed loop system, enabling high water flow and consistent pressure, making them ideal for larger households or commercial setups. Non-pressurized systems rely on gravity and natural convection, making them simpler and typically more affordable, suitable for smaller applications or areas with low water demand. Both types harness solar energy to efficiently heat water, reducing reliance on conventional energy sources.</p></p>

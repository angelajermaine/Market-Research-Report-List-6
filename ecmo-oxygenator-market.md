<p><p>The ECMO (Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation) oxygenator market is primarily divided into two types: V-V-ECMO and V-A-ECMO oxygenators. V-V-ECMO oxygenators are designed for patients with respiratory failure, facilitating gas exchange without affecting cardiac function. In contrast, V-A-ECMO oxygenators support both cardiac and respiratory functions, often used in cases of severe cardiac and respiratory failure. Each type addresses specific clinical needs, impacting their design, configuration, and application in various medical scenarios.</p></p>

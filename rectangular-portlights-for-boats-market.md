<p><p>Rectangular portlights for boats are categorized into two primary types: opening and standard. Opening portlights feature a hinged or sliding mechanism, allowing for ventilation and natural light while ensuring water resistance when closed. These are ideal for areas requiring airflow. On the other hand, standard portlights are fixed units that provide light and aesthetic appeal without the option for opening. They are often used in places where constant ventilation is not a necessity, emphasizing durability and design.</p></p>

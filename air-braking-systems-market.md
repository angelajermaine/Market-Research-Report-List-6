<p><ul><li>Brakeshoe Brake System</li><li>Disc Brake System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air braking systems market encompasses two primary types: the brakeshoe brake system and the disc brake system. The brakeshoe brake system uses friction from shoes pressing against a drum to halt vehicles, offering simplicity and reliability, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Conversely, the disc brake system employs a disc and caliper mechanism for enhanced stopping power, heat dissipation, and performance, making it prevalent in both commercial and passenger vehicles. Both systems are critical for ensuring safety and efficacy in braking performance.</p></p>

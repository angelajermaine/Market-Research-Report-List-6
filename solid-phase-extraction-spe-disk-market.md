<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Solid Phase Extraction SPE Disk Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reversed-Phase Solid phase extraction (SPE)</li><li>Ion-Exchange Solid phase extraction (SPE)</li><li>Normal Phase Solid phase extraction (SPE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solid Phase Extraction (SPE) disk market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Reversed-Phase SPE** targets non-polar compounds using stationary phases that are hydrophobic, suitable for separating organic materials from samples.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Ion-Exchange SPE** utilizes charged stationary phases to selectively adsorb ions based on their charge, ideal for analyzing ionic species in various matrices.</p><p>3. **Normal Phase SPE** employs polar stationary phases, separating compounds based on their polarity, often used for purifying and isolating polar compounds from samples. Each type serves distinct analytical needs across various industries.</p></p>

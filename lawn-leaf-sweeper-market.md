<p><p>The lawn leaf sweeper market comprises primarily two types: push-along sweepers and tow-behind sweepers. Push-along sweepers are manually operated, using a handle that allows users to control the unit while walking behind it, making them ideal for smaller yards. In contrast, tow-behind sweepers attach to a lawn tractor or rider, effectively covering larger areas and saving time and effort. Both types enhance yard maintenance by efficiently collecting leaves and debris, catering to varying user needs based on garden size and preference.</p></p>

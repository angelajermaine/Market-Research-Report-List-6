<p><strong>The Environmental Site Assessment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phase 1</li><li>Phase 2</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Environmental Site Assessments (ESAs) consist of two primary market types. Phase 1 ESA involves a systematic review of a property to identify potential contamination risks through historical, physical, and regulatory evaluations. This phase is essential for assessing environmental liability before property transactions. Phase 2 ESA follows if Phase 1 indicates potential issues, involving detailed sampling and testing of soil, groundwater, and other media to confirm contamination presence and assess its extent. Both phases are crucial for informed decision-making in real estate and development.</p></p>

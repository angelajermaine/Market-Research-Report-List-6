<p><p>The Incline Friction Coefficient Tester market is segmented into two main types: Direct Tests and Indirect Tests. Direct Tests involve evaluating the coefficient of friction by measuring the force required to move an object down an inclined surface, providing immediate and accurate results. Indirect Tests, on the other hand, derive friction coefficients through calculations based on observed behaviors or characteristics of materials under specific conditions, offering a less direct but complementary analysis for friction assessments.</p></p>

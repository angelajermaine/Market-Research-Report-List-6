<p><strong>The Roof Garden Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intensive Roof Garden</li><li>Extensive Roof Garden</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roof garden market can be categorized into two main types: intensive and extensive roof gardens. Intensive roof gardens are characterized by deep soil layers that support a wide variety of plants, including trees, shrubs, and flowers, often requiring significant maintenance and irrigation. They offer recreational spaces and can serve as urban farms. In contrast, extensive roof gardens involve shallow soil, designed for low-maintenance, drought-resistant plants like sedums. They are lighter and more cost-effective, focusing on insulation and stormwater management rather than recreational use.</p></p>

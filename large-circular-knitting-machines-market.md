<p><p>The large circular knitting machines market is divided into two primary types: single circular knitting machines and double circular knitting machines. Single circular knitting machines utilize one set of needles to produce tubular fabric efficiently, ideal for lightweight garments and seamless products. In contrast, double circular knitting machines feature two sets of needles, allowing for more complex structures and a wider range of fabric weights and textures. Both types cater to diverse textile applications in the garment industry, enhancing production capabilities.</p></p>

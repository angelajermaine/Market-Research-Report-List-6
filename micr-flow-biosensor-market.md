Conversely, pressure microcatheters are specialized devices designed for blood pressure monitoring, providing real-time data on hemodynamic parameters within vascular systems. Both types enhance diagnostic accuracy and facilitate early disease detection, driving innovation in healthcare and personalized medicine.</p></p>

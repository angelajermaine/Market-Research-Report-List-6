<p><p>The Mercury Telluride Detectors market is primarily segmented into two types: photovoltaic and photoconductive detectors. Photovoltaic detectors generate a voltage in response to incident infrared radiation, making them suitable for applications requiring signal conversion without an external power source. In contrast, photoconductive detectors alter their electrical conductivity when exposed to light, allowing for high sensitivity and fast response times, ideal for various scientific and industrial applications. These distinctions cater to diverse operational needs and performance specifications in the detection technologies.</p></p>

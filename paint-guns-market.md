<p><p>The paint gun market can be categorized into three main types: semi-automatic, fully-automatic, and manual. Semi-automatic paint guns offer an efficient balance between speed and control, allowing users to fire one shot per trigger pull. Fully-automatic paint guns, on the other hand, continuously fire paint with a single trigger pull, ideal for high-paced scenarios. Manual paint guns require users to operate them with more hands-on effort, offering precise control but typically resulting in slower rates of fire. Each type caters to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>

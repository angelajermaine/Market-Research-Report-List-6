<p><p>The Old Corrugated Cardboard (OCC) baler market can be categorized into electric and hydraulic types. Electric balers utilize electrical power to compress cardboard, offering quieter operation and lower energy costs, making them suitable for smaller facilities. Hydraulic balers, on the other hand, rely on hydraulic systems for heavy-duty compression, providing greater force and efficiency for larger operations. Both types enhance waste management by compacting OCC into manageable bales, optimizing storage and reducing transportation costs in recycling processes.</p></p>

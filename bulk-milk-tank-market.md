<p><p>The bulk milk tank market comprises three primary types based on design and functionality. Horizontal closed tanks are designed for efficient storage and easy transportation of milk, promoting uniform temperature control. Silo or vertical closed tanks maximize space utilization while maintaining hygiene and insulation, ideal for larger dairy operations. Open tanks, while less common, facilitate easy access for cleaning and maintenance, but require more stringent hygiene measures. Each type serves specific operational needs in the dairy industry.</p></p>

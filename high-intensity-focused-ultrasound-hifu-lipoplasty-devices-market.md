<p><p>High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) lipoplasty devices are categorized into two main types: ultrasound-guided and MR-guided. Ultrasound-guided devices utilize real-time ultrasound imaging to precisely target fatty tissues, ensuring effective treatment while minimizing damage to surrounding areas. In contrast, MR-guided devices leverage magnetic resonance imaging for enhanced precision, allowing for detailed visualization of tissues before and during the procedure. Both types aim to improve the efficacy and safety of non-invasive fat reduction treatments, catering to varying patient needs.</p></p>

<p><p>AC drives are categorized into low voltage (LV) and medium voltage (MV) markets based on their operational voltage levels. Low voltage drives typically operate below 1000 volts, widely used in commercial and residential applications for controlling motors in HVAC systems, pumps, and conveyors. Medium voltage drives, ranging from 1 kV to 10 kV, cater to industrial applications, such as mining and energy production, providing enhanced efficiency and performance for larger motors. Both types improve energy management and operational efficiency.</p></p>

Insect viruses, such as baculoviruses, infect and kill specific insects, reducing the need for chemical pesticides. Plant extracts, derived from botanical sources, possess insecticidal properties and act as natural repellents or deterrents. Together, these bioinsecticides promote sustainable agriculture by providing environmentally friendly pest control alternatives.</p></p>

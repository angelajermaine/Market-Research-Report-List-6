<p><p>The light-curing adhesives market comprises several types, including water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based adhesives utilize a water solvent, offering low toxicity and easy cleanup, making them ideal for applications requiring quick curing and minimal environmental impact. In contrast, solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding for demanding applications but raising health and environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds. Both types are increasingly employed in industries such as automotive, electronics, and packaging for their efficient and reliable bonding properties.</p></p>

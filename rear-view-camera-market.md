<p><p>The rear view camera market is primarily divided into two types: wireless and wired rear view cameras. Wireless rear view cameras utilize a transmitter that sends video signals to a monitor without the need for cables, offering ease of installation and flexibility. In contrast, wired rear view cameras connect directly to the vehicle's electrical system and display monitor through cables, providing a stable connection with minimal interference. Both options enhance safety by improving visibility while reversing, catering to different consumer preferences.</p></p>

<p><p>Point-of-Use Abatement Systems are categorized into four main types based on their technology and application. Combustion Type systems use high temperatures to break down pollutants. Wet Type systems utilize scrubbing liquids to capture and neutralize harmful emissions. Dry Type systems employ absorption materials for pollutant removal without liquids, while Catalytic Type systems facilitate chemical reactions to convert harmful substances into less harmful ones. Each type targets specific pollutants and is selected based on environmental and operational needs.</p></p>
